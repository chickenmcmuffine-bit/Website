Interesting Facts About Diamond Pythons


Three Facts


  • Fact 1: Pattern

    Diamond pythons have a beige and black diamond pattern along their body that gives them amazing camouflage in their natural habitat of eastern Australia.

  • Fact 2: Non-Venomous Constrictors

    Unlike venomous snakes, diamond pythons are non-venomous, they kill their prey by wrapping around them and squeezing, making them safe to be a pet compared to venomous species.

  • Fact 3: Size

    Diamond pythons can grow up to 3 meters in length, making them one of the largest snake species in Australia.
