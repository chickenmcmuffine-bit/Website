Diamond python vs Carpet python, which is better?

the diamond python is better

Key Differences

  • Diamond Python: An amazing pattern on the back of its scales. The snake is named after it, its a diamond shaped pattern usually in beige
  • Carpet Python: No distinct pattern, often distorted and less visable, the colours usually blend together

  • Diamond Python: Only in southeaster Australia, making is a lot more saught after and rare
  • Carpet Python: Widespread across Australia and New Guinea, much more common, not saught after

  • Diamond Python: Stays in cooler climates, really good at it, and adapts to other enviornments really well
  • Carpet Python: General species, lives in any climate, not good at any of them

Verdict

Diamond Pythons are much better in terms of everything and the carpet python doesnt stand a chance
